Disarmament and International Security Committee

The committee for Disarmament and International Security – DISEC, was created with the purpose of establishing ‘general principles of cooperation in the maintenance of international peace and security, including the principles governing disarmament and the regulation of armaments’. Keeping in mind the turmoil the worlds finds itself in, the DISEC has never been more intrinsic to international peace and order.

Agenda: Criminal accountability of United Nations Officials and Experts on Mission
We meet at the GA this year to create an accountability mechanism for all UN forces and experts, and to ensure that those who are sent to protect, do. There have been forceful calls internationally for the empowerment of UN Peacekeeping Forces and Experts, in view of the reluctance of sovereign powers to enter active war zones, to ensure that there remains some modicum of control over violence ridden areas. However, what we often forget is, who watches the watchmen?
