Registrations

Dear Delegates,
It is my pleasure to invite you to the Second National Law Institute University Model United Nations Conference (NLIUMUN) organized by the Centre for Research in International Law on 13th and 14th February 2016.

The NLIUMUN is a platform that fosters debate and cooperation amongst delegates from all over the country. Over this weekend, delegates will have the opportunity to participate in a wide spectrum of committees, ranging from the more traditional Disarmament and International Security Committee, to the niche Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice Committee. Along with this, we have put together a well thought out set of agendas and an extremely competent executive board. The focus of this conference is to provide the best possible individual delegate experience.

On behalf of the Organizing Committee and Executive Board, I promise to deliver a great conference experience. However, the success of this conference is also largely dependent on the enthusiasm and preparedness of the delegates. It is therefore my sincere request to you all to join hands with us in raising the standard of the Conference.

Good Luck!

Varsha Sriram
Secretary General
